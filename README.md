Project Overview

Find shared spaces, explore student deals, and discover what's around you, all in one place.

Project Name: StudentConnect
Developer: Jaini Shah
Supervisor/Professor: Ray Toal
Date: 9 December, 2024
Version: 1.0
StudentConnect is an innovative web application designed to simplify student life by connecting them through apartment listings and a marketplace for sharing items. The platform fosters collaboration, sustainability, and community engagement, offering students a streamlined way to find housing, share resources, and thrive academically and personally.

Core Features

Student Dashboard: Personalized dashboards for students to manage activities.
Apartment Listings: Discover and share apartment listings with filters for school, budget, and preferences, and connect with peers for student housing opportunities.
Marketplace: Virtual space for students to share or sell items like books and electronics, browse listings with advanced filters, and promote sustainable reuse within the community.
Mission and Vision

Mission: Empower students by connecting them through apartment listings and a marketplace for sharing items, fostering collaboration and sustainability.
Vision: A future where students easily find housing, share resources, and build meaningful connections to thrive academically and personally.
